About Us

Founded in October 2023, Z.I.R.A. is a relatively new firm backed by over 9 years of experience in industrial automation. Our team consists of hardworking, professional, and adaptable experts dedicated to delivering top-tier engineering solutions. In 2025, we welcomed new talent to further enhance our capabilities and drive innovation. At Z.I.R.A., we embrace change, continuously evolving with industry advancements to provide efficient, precise, and cutting-edge automation solutions.

We Provide Professional Services.

At Z.I.R.A., we provide expert programming and engineering solutions for industrial automation. Our team specializes in industrial robot programming, PLC programming, and advanced system simulations, ensuring seamless integration and optimization across various industries.

We work with all types of robots, with extensive experience in Yaskawa, ABB, Fanuc, Mitsubishi, Omron, and Universal Robots (UR). Additionally, our PLC expertise includes Siemens, Schneider, and Proface, but we are always eager to work with, learn, and adapt to different types of PLCs to meet the unique needs of our clients.

To ensure smooth operation and support, we also offer reliable remote call assistance, helping businesses troubleshoot and optimize their automation systems efficiently. Moreover, we provide on-site programming and debugging for real-time system adjustments, as well as offline programming to develop and test automation solutions before deployment.

At Z.I.R.A., we are committed to delivering flexible, precise, and cutting-edge automation solutions that drive industrial success.
